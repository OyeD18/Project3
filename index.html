ReBrand It!

Creating a Visual Identity Branding Campaign

Role

Graphics designer/Animator – I was in charege of making the visual mockups as well as the first few ittarations of the package design before it was refined. I also made some of the motion graphics and the 10-20 seconds ad for the social campaign.

Scope

6 Weeks & 6 Days, January 21, 2025 to March 10, 2025.

Tools

Adobe Illustrate, Adobe Express, Adobe Animate, Autodesk Sketbook

Background

This project was focused on brand revitalization, mimicking real-world branding workflows used by agencies. The objective was to analyze an existing brand, identify its weaknesses, and execute a full-scale rebrand—both strategically and visually.

The project was divided into three phases:

  1. Strategic Branding: Identifying brand shortcomings and establishing a refreshed brand concept.
  2. Visual Identity Design: Crafting a comprehensive design system, including logo, typography, packaging, and marketing materials.
  3. Marketing & Campaign Execution: Implementing the new identity across social media and video advertising.

The selected brand had to be a physical consumer product (not digital-only) that had visible branding issues, offering an opportunity for meaningful improvement.

Design Process

Phase 1: Brand Audit & Strategy Development
  • Conducted a brand analysis to assess the company’s positioning, audience perception, and visual presence.
  • Researched competitor brands to identify best practices and areas for differentiation.
  • Created insight-driven strategy boards outlining opportunities for repositioning and improvement.

Phase 2: Rebranding & Visual Identity System
  • Developed a core brand concept with a compelling story and strategic positioning.
  • Designed a new logo, typography, and color system aligned with the brand’s new direction.
  • Created mockups for packaging, digital presence, and marketing materials to demonstrate real-world application.

Phase 3: Marketing & Implementation
  • Created a 10-20 second ad campaign utilizing motion graphics and/or live-action footage.
  • Designed social media content (Reels, TikToks, Instagram posts, YouTube shorts) to engage target audiences.
  • Compiled all elements into a final branding package for portfolio presentation.

Takeways

This project strengthened my ability to develop a brand while working within real-world constraints. The biggest challenge my partner and I faced was designing the new packaging and logo. While we enjoyed experimenting with different iterations, they often didn’t turn out as we had envisioned. In the end, we spent nearly half a day refining our designs, mixing and matching different elements from previous versions to create a cohesive final look.

Through this process, I learned how important it is to be adaptable in design, and understanding how to balance creativity with practical execution.
